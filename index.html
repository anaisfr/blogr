Introducing and extensible editor

Blogr features an exeedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing; creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images,videos, ans Markdown. Extensibilty with plugins and themes provide easy ways to add functionality or change the looks of a blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality. Your're in full control

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speed in mind, wordlwide data centers provide the backbone or ultra-connectivity. This ensure your site will load instatly, no matter where your readers are, keepping your site competitive.

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration; third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.

Powerful tooling

Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and deployement a breeze; but capable of producing even the most complicated sites.
